Girls Program Too:
WCS

WCS (Women in Computer Science) or WIC is meant to help MTSU (Middle Tennessee State University) female students in technical majors/minors make connections and acquire skills that will help them in their field of choice.

Coding & Design

Currently we have a few projects our club is working on. We are in the process of coding and designing our new official website as well as a mobile application meant for newbies in Computer Science.

It's not a mystery
and it's not a "Boys Only" thing.

Ladies don't be afraid to join us. Your major doesn't have to be Computer Science. If you're just interested in learning some skills to tweak or create a website or more, we'll be more than happy to teach you and learn with you.
